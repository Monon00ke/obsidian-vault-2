# IELTS Writing — Informal Letter

## Structure

1. **Greeting:** Dear [Name],
2. **Opening:** Thanks for your letter / I was glad to get a letter from you again + Comment
3. **Purpose:** I would be happy to tell you about...
4. **Answer questions 1 & 2**
5. **Answer question 3**
6. **Closing:** hope for future contacts (Do ask if there's anything else you'd like to know)
7. **Sign-off:** Best wishes / All the best / kisses / hugs

> **Word count:** ~140 words

---

## Example 1 — Camping Advice

Dear Sophia,

Thanks for your letter! I was so happy to hear from you again – your idea to try camping this summer sounds really cool and fun!

I'm happy to give you some advice since it's your first time.

I think you'll really like camping because you love nature and quiet spots. Picture this: you wake up to birds singing, drink coffee in the fresh air, cook easy food on the fire and look at the stars at night. It's the best way to forget about the city, phones and all the stress – you always say you need a break like that. And you're quite brave, so putting up a tent or starting a fire will feel fun and make you proud.

But there are some bad sides too. It can be cold at night, even in summer. Sleeping on the ground is not very comfy. Mosquitoes bite a lot, and if it rains, everything gets wet and awful. No hot shower, no Wi-Fi, and you have to plan all the food and stuff yourself.

Still, I'm sure you'll enjoy it if you pick a good campsite. And yes – I'd love to go camping with you this summer! It will be way more fun together. We can put up the tent, cook, laugh when something goes wrong and make nice memories.

Write me if you want help to plan it!

Lots of hugs,
Stepan

## Example 2 — University Recommendation

Dear David,

I was happy to hear that you want to apply for the same course I did at the Smolensk State University. I really liked studying there and I think you will like it too.

I studied at Smolensk State University for two years. We had lessons on math, physics, philosophy, foreign languages. In the last year we could choose some subjects ourselves. I also tried to get a place in a company, which is connected with our future profession. That was a great experience.

I recommend this university because the teachers are good and helpful. The university has modern buildings and strong connections with many companies. This helps students find good internships and jobs after graduation. In the future the new campus will give us more places to make experiments in physics and engineering.

If you want to apply, start writing your personal statement early. Write honestly about why you like math and add any work or school experience you have. Ask your teachers for good references. Check the application deadlines on the university website and do not wait until the last moment.

Write to me if you need more help!

Best wishes,
Stepan
