# IELTS Writing — Formal Letter

## Structure

### 1. Opening + Purpose
**Dear Sir/Madam,** (or **Dear Mr Smith, / Dear Ms Johnson,** — если имя известно)

- I am writing to express my concern regarding… / to enquire about… / to apply for… / to inform you that…
- I was pleased to receive your recent letter / advertisement / invitation, and I would like to respond accordingly.
- Your proposal / suggestion / request regarding … seems most interesting / reasonable / worthy of consideration.

### 2. Transition to Body
- I would be grateful if you could allow me to provide some further details / explain the situation.
- Allow me to outline the circumstances / provide the necessary information as follows:

### 3. Answering Task Points
**First point:**
- With regard to your first point / Regarding the first matter you raised…

**Second point:**
- Turning to your second question / As for the second issue… I would like to clarify that… / I wish to draw your attention to the fact that…

**Third point / Additional details:**
- Concerning your final point / In relation to the third aspect…
- Additionally, I would like to mention…
- I should also point out that…
- Should you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact me.

### 4. Closing
- I would appreciate it if you could give this matter your prompt attention / kindly consider my request at your earliest convenience.
- I look forward to receiving your reply / hearing from you in due course / your favourable response.
- Should you require any additional details, please feel free to contact me at your convenience.

### 5. Sign-off
- **Yours faithfully,** (если начали с Dear Sir/Madam)
- **Yours sincerely,** (если знаете имя: Dear Mr/Ms + фамилия)

## Useful Phrases

### Opening
| Phrase | Use |
|--------|-----|
| I am writing to inform you that … | notification |
| I am writing to enquire about… | asking |
| I am writing with regard to… | reference |
| I am writing in connection with... | reference |
| I am writing with reference to … | reference |
| I would like to describe my situation … | explaining |

### Body — Apologies & Complaints
| Phrase | Use |
|--------|-----|
| I would like to convey my sincere apologies… | apologise |
| I'd like to apologise for… | apologise |
| Please accept my apologies… | apologise |
| I am really disappointed with your service… | complain |
| I'm writing to complain about… | complain |
| I'd like to let you know that I am not satisfied with… | complain |

### Body — Suggestions & Recommendations
| Phrase | Use |
|--------|-----|
| I would like to suggest you… | suggest |
| I'm happy to recommend this to you… | recommend |
| I guess it would be best for you… | advise |
| I'd like to suggest something to you… | suggest |

### Body — Reasons & Causes
| Phrase | Use |
|--------|-----|
| The reason behind this incident is… | explain cause |
| This is due to the… | explain cause |
| This happened owing to the… | explain cause |
| This occurred as a result of… | explain cause |

### Body — Requests & Thanks
| Phrase | Use |
|--------|-----|
| I'm writing to request you… | request |
| I would like to ask for help regarding… | request |
| I'd be thankful if you could do this… | request |
| I would like to thank you for… | thank |
| I am grateful to you… | thank |
| I'd like to express my satisfaction about… | satisfaction |

### Closing
| Phrase | Use |
|--------|-----|
| Thank you for your attention. | general |
| I hope the situation will be resolved soon. | problem |
| I look forward to hearing from you. | general |
| Thank you for your cooperation. | general |
| If you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact me. | general |
