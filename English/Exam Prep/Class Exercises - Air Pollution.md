## Ex.2 — Translate Sentences

1. **Exhaust fumes from cars are the main cause of bad air quality.**
   Выхлопные газы автомобилей — главная причина плохого качества воздуха.

2. **Nitrogen oxides are major components of smog.**
   Оксиды азота являются основными компонентами смога.

3. **Photochemical smog is worst in traffic-congested cities.**
   Фотохимический смог наиболее сильный в городах с интенсивным движением.

4. **Photochemical smog is common in developing countries.**
   Фотохимический смог распространён в развивающихся странах.

5. **Some overpopulated cities suffer from allergies and coughs.**
   Некоторые перенаселённые города страдают от аллергии и кашля.

6. **Air contains minute solid particles.**
   Воздух содержит мельчайшие твёрдые частицы.

7. **Some particles are toxic and can contain lead.**
   Некоторые частицы токсичны и могут содержать свинец.

8. **Today more cars are made to run on unleaded gasoline.**
   Сегодня всё больше автомобилей работают на неэтилированном бензине.

## Ex.3 — Answer the Questions

1. **What dangerous substances do car exhausts contain?**
   Car exhausts contain carbon monoxide, nitrogen oxides, hydrocarbons and lead.

2. **What process is CO produced by?**
   CO is produced by incomplete combustion of fuel.

3. **What part do car emissions play in formation of ground level ozone?**
   Hydrocarbons and nitrogen oxides from car exhausts combine in sunlight and produce ozone.

4. **What can be done to reduce air pollution caused by cars?**
   People can use unleaded petrol, improve engines, reduce traffic and use public transport.

## Ex.4 — Main Ideas

- **Part 1:** The number of cars is increasing every year and this leads to increasing air pollution.
- **Part 2:** Exhaust fumes from cars are the main source of bad air quality which can cause many human illnesses.
- **Part 3:** Photochemical smog and ozone are serious air pollutants, especially in overcrowded cities and developing countries.
- **Part 4:** Lead and toxic particles from petrol are dangerous for human health.

## Ex.10 — Discussion Answers

1. **The main air pollutants in my city?**
   Exhaust fumes and industrial smoke. They come from cars and factories.

2. **What can bad air quality cause?**
   Coughing, allergies and breathing problems.

3. **What should governments do?**
   Reduce traffic, improve public transport and plant more trees.

4. **What do many countries do?**
   Use electric buses, bike lanes and stricter environmental standards.

5. **What should our city do?**
   Reduce the number of old cars and improve public transport.

6. **Can individuals help?**
   Yes. Use bicycles, public transport and avoid unnecessary car trips.
