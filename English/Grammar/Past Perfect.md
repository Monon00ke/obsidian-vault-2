# Past Perfect

## Formula

| | Structure | Example |
|---|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + had + V3 | I **had finished** before he arrived. |
| **Negative** | Subject + had not + V3 | She **had not seen** that film. |
| **Question** | Had + Subject + V3? | **Had** you **eaten** before leaving? |

## When to Use

| Use Case | Example |
|----------|---------|
| **Action completed before another past action** (прошедшее до прошедшего) | When I **arrived**, the train **had already left**. |
| **Cause & effect in the past** | She **failed** because she **hadn't studied**. |
| **Past unreal situations** (wish / if only) | I wish I **had known** about it earlier. |

## Past Perfect vs Past Simple

| Past Simple (chronological) | Past Perfect (earlier) |
|-----------------------------|------------------------|
| I **arrived** at the station. | The train **had left** (before I arrived). |
| He **saw** the movie. | He **had already seen** the book. |
| She **finished** work. | She **had prepared** everything (before finishing). |

## Order of Events

> **Past Perfect** ⇨ более раннее событие
> **Past Simple** ⇨ более позднее событие

| Earlier (Past Perfect) | Later (Past Simple) |
|------------------------|---------------------|
| I **had eaten** dinner | when my friend **arrived**. |
| She **had finished** her homework | before she **went** out. |
| He **had never seen** snow | before he **moved** to Russia. |
| After I **had locked** the door | I **realised** I left my keys inside. |

## Examples

- By the time we **got** there, the concert **had already started**.
- She **was** upset because she **had lost** her wallet.
- I **had never flown** before my trip to Thailand.
- After he **had washed** the car, he **sat** down to rest.
- They **hadn't eaten** anything since morning.

## With "wish" and "if only"

| Wish | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| wish + Past Perfect | сожаление о прошлом | I wish I **had studied** harder. |
| if only + Past Perfect | то же, сильнее | If only we **had left** earlier. |

## Signal Words

| Signal | Example |
|--------|---------|
| **by the time** (к тому моменту) | By the time I arrived, she **had left**. |
| **before** (до того как) | He **had finished** before the deadline. |
| **after** (после того как) | After she **had eaten**, she went to bed. |
| **already** (уже) | I **had already** seen it. |
| **never... until/before** (никогда до) | I **had never** tried sushi **before** that day. |
| **when** (когда) | When I called, he **had already gone**. |

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| I **had arrived** first, then she came. (не нужно PP, хронология) | I **arrived** first, then she came. |
| Before I **had arrived**, she **left**. (неправильная вложенность) | Before I **arrived**, she **had left**. |
| **Have you ever visited** before you moved? | **Had** you **ever visited** before you moved? |
| I already **saw** the film when you called. | I **had already seen** the film when you called. |

## Summary: Tenses in Past

| Tense | Use | Example |
|-------|-----|---------|
| **Past Simple** | completed action | I **worked** yesterday. |
| **Past Continuous** | in progress at a past moment | I **was working** at 5 PM. |
| **Past Perfect** | completed before another past action | I **had worked** before he arrived. |
