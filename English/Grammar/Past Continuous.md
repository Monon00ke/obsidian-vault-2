# Past Continuous

## Formula

| | Structure | Example |
|---|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + was/were + V-ing | I **was reading** a book. |
| **Negative** | Subject + was/were not + V-ing | They **were not sleeping**. |
| **Question** | Was/Were + Subject + V-ing? | **Was** she **cooking**? |

## When to Use

| Use Case | Example |
|----------|---------|
| **Action in progress at a specific past moment** | At 8 PM, I **was watching** TV. |
| **Background action interrupted by another action** | I **was walking** when it **started** to rain. |
| **Two parallel actions in progress** | She **was reading** while he **was cooking**. |
| **Background description in a story** | The sun **was shining**, birds **were singing**. |
| **Politeness / tentative requests** | I **was wondering** if you could help me. |
| **Past arrangement that didn't happen** | I **was planning** to call you. |

## Past Continuous vs Past Simple

| Past Continuous (longer action) | Past Simple (shorter action) |
|----------------------------------|------------------------------|
| I **was having** a shower | when the phone **rang**. |
| They **were driving** home | when they **saw** the accident. |
| She **was studying** | when I **arrived**. |

## Examples

- **Specific time:** At 6 o'clock, I **was having** dinner.
- **Interrupted:** He **was running** when he **fell**.
- **Parallel:** They **were talking** and **laughing**.
- **Background:** It **was getting** darker and the wind **was blowing** harder.
- **Politeness:** I **was wondering** if you could lend me $20.

## Same Time — Different Focus

| Past Simple | Past Continuous |
|-------------|-----------------|
| I **cooked** dinner. (complete action) | I **was cooking** dinner. (in progress) |
| She **wrote** a letter. (finished) | She **was writing** a letter. (in progress) |
| It **rained** all day. (complete fact) | It **was raining** all day. (focus on duration) |

## Signal Words

while, when, as, at that moment, at 8 o'clock yesterday, all day yesterday, the whole evening, at this time last year

> **while** + Past Continuous (длительное действие)
> **when** + Past Simple (короткое действие)

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| I **was go** to school. | I **was going** to school. |
| What **did you do** at 8 PM? (если в процессе) | What **were you doing** at 8 PM? |
| She **wasn't go** to the party. | She **wasn't going** to the party. |
| **Were** he **sleeping**? | **Was** he **sleeping**? |
