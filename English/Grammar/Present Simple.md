# Present Simple

## Formula

| | Structure | Example |
|---|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + V1 (+s/es for he/she/it) | He **works** in a bank. |
| **Negative** | Subject + do/does not + V1 | She **does not** like coffee. |
| **Question** | Do/Does + Subject + V1? | **Do** you speak English? |

## When to Use

| Use Case | Example |
|----------|---------|
| **Habits & routines** (привычки и рутина) | I **get up** at 7 every day. |
| **Permanent facts & truths** (факты и истины) | Water **boils** at 100°C. |
| **Schedules & timetables** (расписания) | The train **leaves** at 7:30. |
| **Stative verbs** (состояния) | I **believe** you are right. |

## Stative Verbs (не используются в Continuous)

| Category | Verbs |
|----------|-------|
| **Thought** | believe, know, think, understand, remember, forget, mean, realise |
| **Emotion** | love, hate, like, dislike, want, need, prefer, wish |
| **Senses** | see, hear, smell, taste, feel |
| **Possession** | have, own, belong, possess |
| **Other** | be, seem, appear, cost, weigh, contain, depend, matter |

## Examples

- She always **arrives** late.
- The Earth **goes** around the Sun.
- The store **opens** at 9 AM.
- I **don't understand** this question.
- **Does** he **play** tennis?

## Signal Words

always, usually, often, sometimes, never, every day/week/month, on Mondays, generally, seldom, rarely, frequently, occasionally

## 3rd Person Singular Spelling

| Rule | Example |
|------|---------|
| Most verbs: +s | work → **works**, speak → **speaks** |
| -s, -ss, -sh, -ch, -x, -o: +es | go → **goes**, wash → **washes**, fix → **fixes** |
| Consonant + y: -y + ies | study → **studies**, fly → **flies** |
| Vowel + y: +s | play → **plays**, buy → **buys** |
| have → **has** | I have → She **has** |
| do → **does** | I do → He **does** |

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| He **work** hard. | He **works** hard. |
| She **doesn't likes** it. | She **doesn't like** it. |
| **Does** he **works**? | **Does** he **work**? |
| I am **know** the answer. | I **know** the answer. |
