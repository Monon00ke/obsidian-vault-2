# Present Continuous

## Formula

| | Structure | Example |
|---|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + am/is/are + V-ing | I **am reading** a book. |
| **Negative** | Subject + am/is/are not + V-ing | She **is not working** today. |
| **Question** | Am/Is/Are + Subject + V-ing? | **Are** you **coming**? |

## When to Use

| Use Case | Example |
|----------|---------|
| **Action happening right now** (прямо сейчас) | Look! It **is raining**. |
| **Temporary situations** (временные ситуации) | I **am staying** at a hotel this week. |
| **Changes & developing processes** (изменения) | The population **is growing** fast. |
| **Fixed future arrangements** (планы на будущее) | We **are meeting** tomorrow at 6. |
| **Annoyance with "always"** (раздражение) | He **is always losing** his keys! |

## Spelling Rules for -ing

| Rule | Base | V-ing |
|------|------|-------|
| Most verbs: +ing | read → **reading**, play → **playing** |
| -e: remove e + ing | make → **making**, write → **writing** |
| -ie: -ie + ying | lie → **lying**, die → **dying** |
| CVC* double final consonant | run → **running**, swim → **swimming** |
| *CVC = consonant-vowel-consonant stressed syllable |

## Dynamic vs Stative Verbs

| Type | Works in Continuous? | Example |
|------|---------------------|---------|
| **Dynamic** (action) | Yes | run, eat, read, work, play |
| **Stative** (state) | No | know, believe, love, hate, want |

> Some verbs change meaning: **I think** (мнение) vs **I am thinking** (процесс)

## Examples

- **Present moment:** She **is cooking** dinner right now.
- **Temporary:** I **am living** with my parents until I find a flat.
- **Change:** More people **are using** electric cars.
- **Future:** They **are flying** to Paris next Monday.
- **Annoyance:** You **are always interrupting** me!

## Signal Words

now, right now, at the moment, currently, these days, tonight, this week/month, today, nowadays, still, look!, listen!

## Present Simple vs Present Continuous

| Present Simple | Present Continuous |
|----------------|-------------------|
| **Permanent** — I **work** in a bank. | **Temporary** — I **am working** on a project. |
| **Habits** — She **drinks** coffee every morning. | **Right now** — She **is drinking** coffee. |
| **General truth** — Water **boils** at 100°C. | **Change** — The water **is boiling**! |
| **Schedule** — The train **leaves** at 7. | **Arrangement** — I **am leaving** tomorrow. |
