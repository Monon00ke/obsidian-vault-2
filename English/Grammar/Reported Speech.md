# Reported Speech (Косвенная речь)

## Direct vs Reported

| Direct speech (прямая речь) | Reported speech (косвенная речь) |
|-----------------------------|----------------------------------|
| He said, **"I am tired."** | He said **(that)** he **was** tired. |
| She asked, **"Do you like it?"** | She asked if I **liked** it. |
| He said, **"I will come."** | He said he **would** come. |

## 1. Tense Shift (Сдвиг времён — Backshift)

Если глагол в главной части в **прошедшем времени** (said, told, asked), время в придаточной части сдвигается:

| Direct | Reported | Change |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Present Simple** → | **Past Simple** | work → **worked** |
| **Present Continuous** → | **Past Continuous** | am working → **was working** |
| **Present Perfect** → | **Past Perfect** | have worked → **had worked** |
| **Past Simple** → | **Past Perfect** | worked → **had worked** |
| **will** → | **would** | will come → **would** come |
| **am/is/are going to** → | **was/were going to** | are going → **were going** |
| **can** → | **could** | can help → **could** help |
| **must** → | **had to** | must go → **had to** go |
| **may** → | **might** | may be → **might** be |
| **shall** → | **should** | shall we → **should** we |

### No shift (не меняется):

| Situation | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **General truth** (всегда верно) | He said water **boils** at 100°C. |
| **Still true now** (актуально сейчас) | She said she **loves** me. (still true) |
| **Past Perfect doesn't change** | He said he **had already eaten**. |
| **could, might, should, would** don't change | She said she **could** swim. |

## 2. Time & Place Changes (Изменение слов)

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| **now** | **then** / at that moment |
| **today** | **that day** |
| **tonight** | **that night** |
| **yesterday** | **the day before** / the previous day |
| **last night** | **the night before** |
| **tomorrow** | **the next day** / the following day |
| **next week** | **the following week** / the week after |
| **ago** | **before** / earlier |
| **here** | **there** |
| **this** | **that** |
| **these** | **those** |
| **come** | **go** |

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| "I will call you **tomorrow**." | He said he would call me **the next day**. |
| "I saw him **yesterday**." | She said she had seen him **the day before**. |
| "I live **here**." | He said he lived **there**. |
| "I bought it **two days ago**." | He said he had bought it **two days before**. |

## 3. Reporting Verbs

### say vs tell

| Verb | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **say** | say + (that) + clause | He said **(that)** he was tired. |
| **say to + object** | say to + person + (that) | He said **to me** (that) he was tired. |
| **tell** | tell + person + (that) | He **told me** (that) he was tired. |

### Other reporting verbs

| Verb | Structure | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **explain** | explain + (that) | He explained **(that)** it was broken. |
| **mention** | mention + (that) | She mentioned **(that)** she was leaving. |
| **admit** | admit + (that) | He admitted **(that)** he was wrong. |
| **claim** | claim + (that) | She claimed **(that)** she saw it. |
| **promise** | promise + (that) | He promised **(that)** he would come. |

## 4. Reported Questions (Косвенные вопросы)

### 4a. Yes/No Questions

**Formula:** asked + **if / whether** + Subject + Verb (no question word order!)

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| "Do you like coffee?" | She asked if I **liked** coffee. |
| "Are you coming?" | He asked if I **was coming**. |
| "Have you seen it?" | They asked if I **had seen** it. |

> ⚠️ **Word order changes:** more like a statement (Subject + Verb), NOT question order (Verb + Subject)

### 4b. Wh- Questions (who, what, where, when, why, how)

**Formula:** asked + **wh-word** + Subject + Verb

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| "Where do you live?" | He asked where I **lived**. |
| "What are you doing?" | She asked what I **was doing**. |
| "When will you arrive?" | They asked when I **would arrive**. |
| "Why did you leave?" | He asked why I **had left**. |

> In wh-questions, keep the wh-word and change order to Subject + Verb (no do/does/did)

## 5. Reported Commands & Requests (Просьбы и приказания)

| Direct | Reported | Structure |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| "Sit down." | He told me **to sit** down. | told + to V |
| "Don't smoke." | She told me **not to smoke**. | told + not to V |
| "Please help me." | He asked me **to help** him. | asked + to V |

| Verbs | Structure | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **tell** (велеть) | tell + person + to V | He told **me to wait**. |
| **ask** (просить) | ask + person + to V | She asked **him to help**. |
| **order** (приказывать) | order + person + to V | The officer ordered **them to stop**. |
| **warn** (предупреждать) | warn + person + not to V | He warned **us not to go** there. |
| **advise** (советовать) | advise + person + to V | I advised **her to see** a doctor. |
| **remind** (напоминать) | remind + person + to V | She reminded **me to call** her. |

## 6. Reported with Infinitive / -ing

| Direct | Reported | Example |
|--------|----------|---------|
| "Yes, I'll do it." | **agree** + to V | He agreed **to help**. |
| "No, I won't do it." | **refuse** + to V | She refused **to answer**. |
| "I promise I'll come." | **promise** + to V | He promised **to come**. |
| "Let's go." | **suggest** + V-ing | He suggested **going** there. |
| "I'm sorry I broke it." | **apologise** + for V-ing | She apologised **for breaking** it. |
| "I did it." | **admit** + V-ing | He admitted **stealing** it. |
| "I won't do it again." | **deny** + V-ing | She denied **taking** the money. |
