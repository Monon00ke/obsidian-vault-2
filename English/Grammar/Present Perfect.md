# Present Perfect

## Formula

| | Structure | Example |
|---|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + have/has + V3 | I **have finished** my homework. |
| **Negative** | Subject + have/has not + V3 | She **has not arrived** yet. |
| **Question** | Have/Has + Subject + V3? | **Have** you **seen** this film? |

## When to Use

| Use Case | Example |
|----------|---------|
| **Completed action with result now** (результат важен) | I **have lost** my keys. (can't get in now) |
| **Life experience** (ever/never) | She **has visited** Japan twice. |
| **Recent action** (just, already, yet) | He **has just left**. |
| **Action from past continuing to now** (for/since) | I **have lived** here for 5 years. |
| **Unfinished time period** (today, this week) | I **have drank** three coffees today. |
| **First/second/third time** | This is the first time I **have flown**. |

## Signal Words

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **just** | только что | I've **just** eaten. |
| **already** | уже | She's **already** left. |
| **yet** | ещё (не) — вопрос/отрицание | Have you finished **yet**? |
| **ever** | когда-либо | Have you **ever** been to London? |
| **never** | никогда | I've **never** seen that movie. |
| **for** | в течение (длительность) | He's worked here **for** 10 years. |
| **since** | с (отправная точка) | She's lived here **since** 2010. |
| **recently** | недавно | I've **recently** started yoga. |
| **lately** | в последнее время | Have you seen him **lately**? |
| **so far** | пока что | So far, everything **has gone** well. |
| **up to now** | до сих пор | Up to now, we **have received** 50 replies. |

## For vs Since

| for + period of time | since + starting point |
|-----------------------|------------------------|
| for three years | since 2020 |
| for a week | since Monday |
| for a long time | since I was a child |
| for two hours | since 3 o'clock |

## Ever / Never / Just / Already / Yet — Position

- **ever:** Have you **ever** been abroad?
- **never:** I have **never** seen that film. (between have/has and V3)
- **just:** She has **just** arrived.
- **already:** I have **already** finished. (for positive — уже сделал)
- **yet:** Have you done it **yet**? (questions); I haven't done it **yet**. (negatives — at the end)

## Present Perfect vs Past Simple

| Present Perfect | Past Simple |
|-----------------|-------------|
| **Result now:** I've **lost** my keys. | **When?** I **lost** them yesterday. |
| **Experience:** I've **been** to Paris. | **Details:** I **went** there in 2019. |
| **Unfinished time:** I've **drunk** 3 cups today. | **Finished time:** I **drank** 3 cups this morning. |
| **No specific time:** She's **bought** a car. | **Specific time:** She **bought** it last week. |

## Examples

- I **have visited** 15 countries so far.
- She **has never tried** sushi.
- **Have** you **ever met** a famous person?
- They **haven't finished** the project yet.
- He **has worked** here **since** March.
- I've **just heard** the news.
- This is the best meal I **have ever had**.
- It's the third time you **have asked** me that!

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| I **have seen** him **yesterday**. | I **saw** him yesterday. |
| He **has** not **finished** **yet**? | **Has** he not finished yet? |
| She **lives** here since 2015. (present simple) | She **has lived** here since 2015. |
| I **have went** to the store. (went ≠ V3) | I **have gone** to the store. |
| I am **here for 2 hours**. | I **have been** here for 2 hours. |

## Irregular Verbs V3 (some important ones)

| V1 | V2 | V3 | V1 | V2 | V3 |
|----|----|----|----|----|----|
| be | was/were | **been** | go | went | **gone** |
| see | saw | **seen** | take | took | **taken** |
| write | wrote | **written** | speak | spoke | **spoken** |
| eat | ate | **eaten** | break | broke | **broken** |
| drive | drove | **driven** | fall | fell | **fallen** |
| know | knew | **known** | show | showed | **shown** |
