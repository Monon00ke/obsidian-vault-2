# Present Perfect Continuous

## Formula

| | Structure | Example |
|---|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + have/has + been + V-ing | I **have been waiting** for an hour. |
| **Negative** | Subject + have/has not been + V-ing | She **has not been sleeping** well. |
| **Question** | Have/Has + Subject + been + V-ing? | **Have** you **been crying**? |

## When to Use

| Use Case | Example |
|----------|---------|
| **Action started in past, continues to now** (длится до сих пор) | I **have been studying** for three hours. |
| **Action just finished with visible result** (только что закончилось, результат виден) | You look tired. **Have** you **been working**? |
| **Emphasis on duration** (акцент на длительности) | It **has been raining** all day. |
| **Temporary situation** (временная ситуация) | I **have been living** with my parents lately. |

## Present Perfect vs Present Perfect Continuous

| Present Perfect (result) | Present Perfect Continuous (process/duration) |
|---------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| I've **painted** the room. (it's done ✅) | I've **been painting** the room. (I'm still at it) |
| She's **read** the book. (she knows it) | She's **been reading** all afternoon. (focus on activity) |
| How many pages **have** you **written**? (quantity) | How long **have** you **been writing**? (duration) |
| He's **drunk** three cups. (number) | He's **been drinking** coffee all day. (habit/process) |

## Action vs State Verbs

| Can use in PPC (action verbs) | Cannot use in PPC (stative verbs) |
|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| work, run, wait, study, rain, read, write, cook, travel, play | know, believe, love, hate, want, need, belong, seem, appear |

> For stative verbs → use Present Perfect: I've **known** her for years. (NOT I've been knowing)

## Examples

- **Continuing action:** I **have been working** here since 2019.
- **Visible result:** His eyes are red. He **has been crying**.
- **Annoyance:** You **have been using** my shampoo again!
- **Duration:** How long **have** you **been learning** English?
- **Action just finished:** I'm sweaty because I **have been running**.

## Signal Words

| Word | Example |
|------|---------|
| **for** (в течение) | I've been waiting **for** 2 hours. |
| **since** (с) | I've been living here **since** January. |
| **all day/morning/week** | It's been raining **all day**. |
| **how long** (questions) | **How long** have you been working? |
| **recently / lately** | I've been feeling tired **lately**. |
| **the whole** | I've been studying **the whole evening**. |

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| I **have been knowing** her for long. | I **have known** her for long. |
| She **has been working** yesterday. | She **worked** yesterday. |
| I **have been going** to London twice. | I **have been** to London twice. |
| They **have been building** the house. (and it's done) | They **have built** the house. |
| I'm tired because I **worked** all day. (if still ongoing/just finished) | I'm tired because I **have been working** all day. |
