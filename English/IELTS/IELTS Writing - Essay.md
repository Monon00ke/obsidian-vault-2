# IELTS Writing — Essay

> **Word count:** 200–250 words

---

## Example — Domestic Holidays

**Prompt:** In the future, more people will choose to spend their holidays in their own country instead of travelling abroad. Do you agree or disagree?

In the future, more people will choose to spend their holidays in their own country instead of travelling abroad. I fully agree with this idea.

First, holidays abroad are becoming too expensive. Tickets for planes, hotel rooms and meals in other countries cost a lot of money. Many families do not have enough money for that. On the other hand, holidays inside the country are much cheaper. People can go by car or bus and stay in local places without spending too much.

Second, travelling inside the country is much easier. There is no need to get a visa or wait a long time at the airport. People do not have problems with language or strange food. Everything feels comfortable and familiar. For example, many of my friends now prefer to visit lakes, mountains or beaches in our own country rather than fly to other places.

Third, people are starting to worry more about the environment. Flying to other countries creates a lot of pollution from planes. Many people want to protect nature and choose holidays that do not harm the planet so much. Staying in their own country is a good way to do this.

Of course, some people will still want to travel abroad to see new places and learn about other cultures. But most ordinary families will choose holidays at home because it is cheaper, easier and safer.

In conclusion, I believe that in the future more and more people will spend their holidays in their own country. It saves money, makes life simpler and helps the environment. This is a good change for many people.
