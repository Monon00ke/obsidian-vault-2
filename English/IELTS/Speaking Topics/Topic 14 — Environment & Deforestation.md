## Key Points

### Лёгкие планеты

The Amazon rainforest is often called the **lungs of the planet** because it produces about 20% of the world's oxygen. Forests:
- **Absorb CO2** and produce oxygen
- **Regulate** global temperatures
- **Host** 80% of terrestrial biodiversity
- **Provide** livelihoods for millions of people

### Почему вырубают леса

| Cause | Explanation |
|-------|-------------|
| **Agriculture** | Clearing land for farming, cattle ranching |
| **Logging** | Harvesting wood for paper, furniture, construction |
| **Urbanisation** | Building cities, roads, infrastructure |
| **Mining** | Extracting minerals, oil, gas |
| **Palm oil plantations** | One of the biggest drivers globally |

### Последствия

| Consequence | Impact |
|-------------|--------|
| **Climate change** | Less CO2 absorption, more greenhouse gases |
| **Biodiversity loss** | Species lose their habitats, many go extinct |
| **Disrupted water cycle** | Forests regulate rainfall patterns |
| **Soil erosion** | Without tree roots, soil washes away |
| **Indigenous displacement** | Forest peoples lose their homes |
| **Flooding** | Forests absorb excess water |

### Что можно сделать

| Solution | How it helps |
|----------|--------------|
| **Reduce paper use** | Less demand for logging |
| **Choose sustainable products** | Look for FSC-certified wood |
| **Plant trees** | Reforestation projects |
| **Support conservation** | Donate to / volunteer for NGOs |
| **Reduce meat consumption** | Cattle farming is a major driver |
| **Recycle** | Less demand for raw materials |

## Sample Answer — Part 1

> **Examiner:** Are you concerned about the environment?

> **To be honest**, yes, I am very concerned. **In my opinion**, environmental issues like climate change and deforestation are the biggest challenges of our generation.

> **Generally speaking**, I try to do my part — I recycle, avoid single-use plastics, and use public transport. **However**, I know that individual actions are not enough — we need systemic change.

## Sample Answer — Part 3

> **Examiner:** Why are rainforests important for the planet?

> **Without a doubt**, rainforests are crucial for the survival of our planet. They are often called **the lungs of the planet** because they absorb enormous amounts of CO2 and produce oxygen. **Moreover**, they are home to millions of species and indigenous communities.

> **To give you an example**, the Amazon rainforest alone produces about 20% of the world's oxygen. If we continue **deforestation** at the current rate, we will lose this vital resource.

> **In my opinion**, governments should do much more to protect forests. **Furthermore**, consumers in developed countries should avoid products linked to deforestation, like unsustainable palm oil or beef from cleared land.

> **At the end of the day**, protecting forests is not just about saving trees — it's about saving ourselves.

## IELTS Writing Examples

### Essay 1 — Deforestation and its consequences

**Prompt:** Deforestation is a serious global issue. What are the main causes and what effects does it have on the environment?

> Deforestation, the large-scale removal of forests, is one of the most pressing environmental challenges of our time. This essay will examine its primary causes and the devastating effects it has on our planet.

> **The main causes of deforestation** are agricultural expansion, logging, and urbanisation. **To give you an example**, vast areas of the Amazon rainforest are cleared every year to create cattle ranches and soy plantations. **Furthermore**, trees are cut down for timber, paper production, and to make space for roads and cities. **Palm oil plantations** are another major driver, particularly in Southeast Asia.

> **The environmental consequences are severe.** Forests are often called **the lungs of the planet** because they absorb CO2 and produce oxygen. When they are destroyed, carbon is released into the atmosphere, accelerating **climate change**. **Moreover**, deforestation destroys **biodiversity** — countless species lose their habitats and face extinction. **Finally**, it disrupts the water cycle, leading to droughts and floods in affected regions.

> **To combat this issue**, governments must enforce stricter laws against illegal logging and support **reforestation** projects. **As consumers**, we can choose products that are certified sustainable and reduce our consumption of paper and beef.

> **In conclusion**, deforestation is a complex problem caused mainly by agriculture and logging, with devastating effects on climate and biodiversity. **Without a doubt**, urgent action is needed to protect the world's forests.

### Letter — Formal: To local government about pollution

> Dear Mayor,

> I am writing to express my concern about the deteriorating air quality in our city, which has become a serious health issue for residents.

> **In recent months**, I have noticed a significant increase in smog and traffic congestion. **As a resident of this city**, I believe that urgent measures are needed to reduce **air pollution** from vehicles and factories.

> **I would like to suggest** the following actions: expanding the network of bike lanes, improving public transport, and introducing stricter emission standards for factories. **Furthermore**, planting more trees along main roads would help absorb CO2 and improve **air quality**.

> **I hope you will consider** these suggestions. **I look forward to** seeing positive changes in our city.

> Yours faithfully,
> [Your Name]
